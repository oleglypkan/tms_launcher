Opening SoftDev issues

SoftDev issues and other SoftDev entities such as change requests, activities and so on can be opened by TMS Launcher in WebSoftDev using special link.
SoftDev entities are recognized by their special format that differs from tasks, defects, etc.
Example of the issue: ISS-DEVG-20183IT
Hotkeys and browser settings used to open issues are configured on URLs page of settings window. However, special WebSoftDev link cannot be changed.
